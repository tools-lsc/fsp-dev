F.A.Q.

Is FSP a payment processor or a marketing platform?
We are a Revenue Operations Platform that combines both. We replace your fragmented stack—payments, loyalty, and fan engagement—with a single operating system. This allows you to lower operational costs while simultaneously unlocking new revenue streams from participation and sponsorship.
How does this improve my bottom line?
We impact your P&L in three ways:
Reduced Costs: Our modern financial rails significantly lower your merchant transaction fees compared to legacy processors.
New Revenue: You earn a share of platform revenue generated by your patrons' participation in global challenges.
Increased LTV: Our integrated rewards network drives higher retention and frequency without requiring you to discount your own inventory.
What is the "FSP Network Effect"?
Unlike standalone software, FSP connects your venue or league to a global ecosystem. A participant in your venue isn't just competing locally; they are entering a global pool. This turns your local venue into a "Qualifier Node" for major events, driving traffic and engagement that standalone venues cannot replicate.
How do you offer such high-value rewards without charging us?
We utilize a Network-Funded Model. Because FSP operates at a global scale with major partners, we pool resources to fund "Inner Circle" experiences (like VIP trips and signed memorabilia). You get to offer these enterprise-grade perks to your customers as a benefit of being an FSP partner, without carrying the cost on your balance sheet.
Do we need to rip and replace our existing hardware?
No. FSP is designed as an Overlay Layer.
Payments: We offer "Side-Car" solutions that sit alongside your existing POS to accept lower-fee transactions instantly.
Vision AI: We are hardware agnostic. In most cases, we can utilize your existing security infrastructure or simple consumer-grade devices to run our Vision Stack.
Can I control the incentives?
Absolutely. Our Programmable Loyalty engine allows you to set specific goals—like driving footfall on off-peak days or increasing F&B spend. You set the target; our system automates the reward delivery.
How long does integration take?
Our digital onboarding allows you to be up and running with payments and basic rewards in minutes. Full Vision AI integration varies by venue size, but our "Light" implementation requires zero downtime.
How does settlement work?
We offer flexible settlement options designed for modern business needs. You can receive funds instantly via our digital rails, or engage our automated sweep function to have funds settled to your traditional bank account in your local currency.
Is this secure?
Security is our core product. We employ institutional-grade identity verification (KYB) and partner with regulated financial custodians to ensure that every transaction and data point is encrypted, compliant, and secure.
