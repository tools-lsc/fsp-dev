F.A.Q.

What is the Movement Economy?
The Movement Economy is FSP's vision where every verified human movement has bankable value. Instead of paying for passive impressions, brands pay for verified actions. Instead of playing for free, athletes earn for every move. FSPx.ai is the clearing house that connects both sides.
How do Movement Creators earn?
Every time you perform a verified action (a bowling strike, a sprint, a tennis ace), brands who have set bounties for those actions pay you instantly through the FSPx.ai clearing house. You can also earn by sharing highlights on social media and growing your pool.
How does AI verification work?
Our Edge Vision Engine uses ballistics tracking, skeletal analysis, and anti-fraud detection to verify human movement in under 100 milliseconds. It works on any camera-enabled device. No bots, no fakes—only real human performance is rewarded.
What is a "Bounty" in the Campaign Manager?
A Bounty is the price a brand sets for a specific verified action. For example, a brand might set a bounty of $1.00 for every verified bowling strike. When a player performs that action, the bounty is automatically paid out through AI verification.
How is this different from traditional advertising?
Traditional ads pay for impressions and clicks that can be faked. FSP's model pays only for verified physical human actions. Your dROAS (direct Return on Ad Spend) is based on real, AI-verified movement—no bots, no wasted spend.
Can I integrate FSP into my existing app?
Yes. The FSP SDK is designed as a plug-and-play module. With a few lines of code, you can enable Edge AI detection and tap into the FSPx.ai clearing house for new revenue. No hardware changes required.
How do venues benefit?
Venues become "Movement Creators" themselves. Every game played under your roof generates verified data that earns revenue. Your own venue pool earns from every session, and AI challenges fill empty slots during off-peak hours.
Is this secure?
Security is our core product. We employ institutional-grade identity verification (KYB/KYC) and partner with regulated financial custodians to ensure that every transaction and data point is encrypted, compliant, and secure.
