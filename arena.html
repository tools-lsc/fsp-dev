An Arena is your own branded competitive environment on FSP. You choose the sports, set the entry fees, configure rewards, and define the economics. Participants discover it through the Explore feed and compete across a season.

Anyone can apply. Most arena creators are academies and clubs, coaches, athletes, or celebrities and influencers. If you already have an audience, you already have a potential arena. Apply through a short in-app process and you can start building immediately.

Arena hosts earn a share of every entry fee paid by participants. You also earn from brand-sponsored challenges, advertiser bounties settled against verified sessions, and SP booster campaigns running inside your arena. Revenue is automated via Stripe — no invoicing, no manual transfers.

Brands buy verified engagement — not impressions. Advertisers can sponsor performance bounties, fund SP boosters, and run campaigns settled against deterministic data from real human movement. Arena hosts control advertiser access: invite, approve, or block.

A Collab Arena is co-owned by two hosts with a shared dashboard and locked economics. Both hosts must approve any mid-season changes. It is designed for partnerships — think two brands, two athletes, or a venue and an influencer running an arena together.

FSP’s proprietary AI system that verifies real human movement in real time using edge computing. It confirms that a real person is performing real athletic activity — purpose-built for sports, not off-the-shelf computer vision.

Captains are appointed by the arena host to recruit participants and grow the arena. They earn from the Captain Pool based on their performance and the activity they generate. High-performing participants can also be promoted into the captain bracket.

Bowling, squash, basketball, baseball hitting, cricket, table tennis, soccer, and water pong — with more in development. 80% of the platform is shared across every sport. One infrastructure, universal plug and play.
