FSP Ads is the first platform where every impression is verified by a real physical action. The Kinetic Truth Engine™ confirms that a real human is performing real athletic activity before any ad is served. This eliminates fraud entirely and provides deterministic — not probabilistic — attribution from session to sale.

A Performance Bounty is a brand-funded challenge settled against verified human activity. For example: "Complete 50 bowling sessions and earn an exclusive brand reward." The brand pays only when the physical activity is confirmed by the Kinetic Truth Engine™ — not when an ad is served.

Every verified session on FSP generates a deterministic data point. When a brand campaign is active during that session, the attribution is direct — no cookies, no pixels, no probabilistic modelling. The closed loop connects the physical session to the brand impression to the downstream purchase behaviour.

Campaigns can be targeted by geography, sport, arena, demographic, engagement tier (captain vs. challenger, gold/silver/bronze), and session frequency. SP Boosters additionally support region-specific and segment-specific targeting.

Programmatic access is coming soon. FSP is building SSP integration so you'll be able to bid on verified sport sessions through your existing DSP alongside your regular digital media buys, with deterministic verification data flowing through the bid stream.

Ad inventory spans all active Arenas globally. Direct Buy advertisers can target specific arenas or sports; self-serve and programmatic buyers can target across the full inventory. Programmatic Ads are coming soon.

Arena hosts have full control over which advertisers operate inside their arena. Hosts can invite, approve, or block any advertiser. Campaign management happens in the Advertiser Dashboard, but placement requires host-side approval.
