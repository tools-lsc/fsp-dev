Get Subsidies from Government Programs or the FFS Foundation

If the Paid Movement Creator license feels out of reach, you still have a path in. FSP partners with public wellness programs and the Foundation for the Future of Sports (FFS) to offset your onboarding cost.

FFS Foundation Subsidy

The Foundation for the Future of Sports underwrites $399 licenses for qualified athletes and venues that are ready to prove their movement.

Section 127 Grants

Employer-sponsored educational assistance programs that can cover qualified sports technology and performance training expenses.

HSA / FSA / LSA

Most Health, Flexible, or Lifestyle Spending Accounts treat FSP verification as eligible activity. File your monthly statement for reimbursement.

International Government Grants

Outside the U.S.? Submit your national sport or youth development grant info and we will help route it directly to your Movement Creator fee.

How It Works
01

Enter a Challenge

Open the app, pick a sport, and record your skill challenge using your phone camera.

02

AI Verifies You

Skeletal tracking and video analysis score your performance — speed, accuracy, trajectory.

03

Climb the Rankings

Your score feeds into a global leaderboard. Top performers qualify for higher tiers and prize pools.

04

Compete Live

The best advance to championship finals in iconic global venues, competing alongside the Captains.

01. Bowling

WORLD'S BEST BOWLER

Vision-AI measures ball speed, rev rate, entry angle, and pocket-hit consistency. Rise the ladder to purpose-built finals lanes; the Top 10 advance to the WBL draft pool.

02. Squash

WORLD'S BEST SQUASH PLAYER

Vision-AI evaluates pace, accuracy, and movement economy in glass-court drills and rally scenarios. Rank globally and step onto a show court for finals; winners get a WPS access path.

03. World’s Best Cricket Player

WORLD'S BEST CRICKET PLAYER

Vision-AI scores shot timing, control, and range against pace and swing. Qualify remotely, climb a global ladder, and reach stadium finals; standouts enter pro showcases.
