Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to make FSP work for you. Here's what we gather:

Athletic Performance & Biometric Data — This is the core of what we do. We collect biometric data and biometric identifiers including but not limited to: skeletal movement data, body positioning and posture analysis, gait and movement patterns, joint angles and biomechanical measurements, speed, velocity, and acceleration metrics, reaction times, heart rate and cardiovascular indicators, facial geometry (for identity verification and anti-spoofing), and any other physiological or biological measurements captured by our Edge AI Technology during verified sessions. Our Edge AI analyzes this in real-time to verify your skills and score your performances.

Account Info — Your name, email, date of birth, and the basics we need to set you up. Plus your login credentials and any preferences you set.

Competition Data — Your scores, rankings, challenge attempts, and how you're progressing through skill levels. If you join celebrity challenges, we track that too.

Device & Technical Info — Standard stuff like your IP address, device type, browser fingerprints, behavioral and interaction data, and how you use the app. This helps us keep things running smoothly.

Location Data — When you use location features, we collect GPS data for outdoor activities and venue positioning for indoor facilities. You control when this is on.

Payment Info — If you make purchases or win prizes, our payment partners handle the transaction details. We only see what we need for records.

Connected Devices — Data from your fitness wearables, smartwatches, and sports equipment when you choose to connect them.

Programmatic Advertising & Telemetry Data — During verified sessions, our system may transmit real-time telemetry data to third-party demand-side platforms (DSPs) and advertising exchanges via programmatic bid requests (including OpenRTB protocols). This telemetry may include your current physical activity type, movement velocity, calorie expenditure, fatigue index, skeletal fidelity score, and AR placement coordinates. This data is used to serve contextual augmented reality advertisements during your sessions. By participating in verified sessions, you irrevocably consent to such real-time data transmission to advertising partners and waive any claims to compensation in connection with FSP's monetization of such data to the fullest extent permitted by law. Where strictly required by applicable mandatory law (including biometric privacy laws such as BIPA, CIPA, and equivalent state or national laws), we will obtain your consent before transmitting biometric telemetry to advertising partners.

Commerce & Attribution Data — When you use our commerce features, we collect receipt images and OCR-extracted data (date, item, vendor) from receipt scans, 3D object and logo recognition data from AR gear authentication scans, GPS and QR code data from Arena Geo-Scan check-ins, and purchase and click data from in-app affiliate commerce links. This data is used for commerce attribution, brand partner CPA verification, and SP minting, and may be shared with brand partners, advertisers, and other third parties for any lawful commercial purpose.

Captain Program & Referral Network Data — If you participate in the Captain Program or referral system, we collect and process your referral chain data (up to three levels), Network Contribution Score (NCS) calculations, downstream participant activity and session data attributed to your referral network, tier placement and promotion/relegation history, and monthly payout records. This data may be used for fraud detection, NCS recalculation, claw back or slashing actions, and for any lawful commercial purpose.

Responsible Gaming Data — If you use our responsible gaming tools, we collect data relating to your spending limits (daily, weekly, monthly), cooling-off periods, self-exclusion elections (30 days, 90 days, 6 months, or permanent), and related behavioral patterns. This data is processed to enforce your responsible gaming preferences and comply with applicable regulations. Self-exclusion data is retained for the duration of the exclusion period plus 7 years for regulatory compliance, and may be shared with regulatory authorities where required by law.

Sweepstakes & Prize Verification Data — If you participate in Golden Ticket sweepstakes or win experience prizes, we collect identity verification data (via Plaid/Stripe Identity or equivalent), government-issued ID, background check results (for in-person experiences), and contact and logistics information for prize fulfillment. This data is processed for KYC compliance, safety verification, and experience coordination, and is retained for 7 years for legal and audit purposes.
