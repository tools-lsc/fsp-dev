How Staking Works? Outcomes are determined by your athletic ability and performance, not chance. You must complete AI Verification before participating. You can only compete in skill categories where you've achieved verified competency.

  • Tiered System. We organize staking into tiers: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Professional, and Elite. Your tier is based on your verified skill level. Each tier has minimum and maximum stake amounts. You advance through demonstrated skill progression.
  • Stakes and Payouts. Stakes are deposited before challenges and held in escrow. Outcomes are determined through real-time AI assessment.   • Set daily, weekly, and monthly spending limits.
  • Mandatory cooling-off periods after significant losses.
  • Self-exclusion options for 30 days, 90 days, 6 months, or permanently.
  • Resources and referrals to professional support services

Not Gambling. The Staking System is skill-based gaming, not gambling. All challenges require demonstrated skill and have performance-based outcomes. We may modify or suspend staking in jurisdictions where regulations conflict with our operations.

Student-Athlete Staking Restrictions. If you are a student-athlete subject to NCAA, NAIA, NJCAA, or high school athletic association rules, your participation in Staking activities may be restricted or prohibited based on applicable NIL laws and institutional policies.   • Deposits and Withdrawals. Deposits are processed through secure payment systems with identity verification. Withdrawals require identity confirmation and may have processing delays for security. We maintain separate accounts for user funds. Currency conversion includes transparent fee disclosure.
  • Peer-to-Peer Wagers. The Platform enables peer-to-peer ("P2P") wagers in which two players escrow an agreed amount of rewards (SP) and compete head-to-head in a verified session within the same sport. Both players' SP is locked in escrow upon acceptance of the challenge and cannot be spent, transferred, or redeemed while escrowed. The higher-scoring player, as determined by AI verification, receives the full SP pot. If either player fails to complete a verified session within the challenge window, that player forfeits and the full pot is awarded to the other player. Full escrow state machine mechanics, retry windows, and edge case rules are set out in the Competition/Challenge Rules.
  • Squad-to-Squad Wagers. Two squads within the same sport may pool SP contributions from their members and compete as groups over a set duration. Winning criteria (aggregate sessions or aggregate score) are set at challenge creation. The winning squad's members split the combined SP pot from both squads. The same escrow mechanics, forfeit rules, and dispute resolution procedures that apply to P2P wagers apply to squad-to-squad wagers at squad scale. Full terms are set out in the Competition/Challenge Rules.
  • Wager Disputes and Edge Cases. If the AI verification system loses tracking fidelity during a wagered session, the affected session may be flagged as inconclusive and the affected player may receive one retry within 24 hours. If both sessions are inconclusive, escrowed SP is returned to both players. Either player may dispute a wager result within 4 hours of settlement, which triggers frame-by-frame re-verification. If the re-verification changes the outcome, the settlement is reversed and re-processed. If the original result stands, it is final. All wager disputes and resolutions are logged on the Platform's immutable ledger.
In short: Staking is skill-based, not gambling. Compete at your verified skill level. P2P and squad-to-squad wagers use SP escrow with automatic settlement and dispute resolution. We offer responsible gaming tools including spending limits and self-exclusion.
