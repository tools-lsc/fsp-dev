F.A.Q.

What is FSP?
FSP is the first global sports league where everyone can play We use Edge AI to verify your skills, rank you on a global leaderboard, and reward you with real prizes, brand bounties, and a path to the pros.
How does the Vision App verify my performance?
Our proprietary Edge AI runs directly on your smartphone. Point your camera at the lane, court, or field and our computer vision tracks ball trajectory, skeletal mechanics, and physics in real-time to produce a Verified Score you can trust.
Can the AI be cheated?
Our Vision Stack uses anti-cheat protocols similar to elite esports. We analyze biomechanics and environmental consistency to flag anomalies. For high-stakes events, finalists must replicate their performance in sanctioned live events.
How do I get drafted for FSP Games?
Qualification is purely meritocratic based on your Skill Score from the Vision App, your community influence, and your leaderboard ranking. Top-ranked athletes are invited to the draft for live, broadcasted events.
What are SPs?
Skill Points unlock access to the Inner Circle of sports. Redeem them for signed gear from our partners, VIP experiences at global events, or Challenge Tickets to enter high-stakes tournaments.
What is a Movement Creator?

Movement Creators are community leaders who onboard participants into their arena. Once you have 30+ members, you unlock revenue sharing, brand ad revenue, and the ability to set earnings distribution within your group.
