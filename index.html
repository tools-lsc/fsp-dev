F.A.Q.

The FSP Ecosystem

Is FSP a bank, a sports league, or a tech company?
We are the financial operating system that powers all three. FSP replaces the fragmented world of sports where your money, your stats, and your rewards are in different places with a unified infrastructure. We use modern financial rails to hold your capital, AI technology to verify your skills, and a global network to provide the competition.
What do you mean by "Infrastructure for Meritocracy"?
Traditional sports rely on gatekeepers: scouts, expensive academies, and politics. FSP relies on data. We built the infrastructure (Edge AI and global leaderboards) to ensure that if you have the talent, you get seen, ranked, and rewarded on a global platform, regardless of where you live or who you know.

The Technology (Vision AI)

How does the "Vision App" verify my performance?
We use proprietary Edge AI technology that runs directly on your smartphone. When you record a session (like bowling a frame or hitting a squash shot), our computer vision models track skeletal movement, ball trajectory, and physics in real-time. This data creates a Verified Score that cannot be faked, allowing you to compete globally from your local venue.
Can the AI be cheated?
Our "Vision Stack" is built with anti-cheat protocols similar to elite esports. We analyze biomechanics and environmental consistency to flag anomalies. Furthermore, to qualify for high-stakes events like the Apex Gauntlet, finalists must replicate their performance in our sanctioned live events.

The Finance (Vault & Stash)

How is the FSP Vault different from a traditional savings account?
Traditional banks use your deposits to fund their own loans, paying you pennies in interest. The FSP Vault is built on stablecoin financial rails, giving you access to institutional-grade yields. Your capital is principal-protected, meaning your initial deposit is secure, while the yield it generates fuels your rewards and competitive journey.
What is an "AthleteStash"?
Think of it as crowdfunding, but better. Instead of asking friends and family to donate money (which they lose), they can deposit money into your AthleteStash. They keep their principal, but the yield generated by that pool is directed to you to fund your training, travel, and gear. It's a sustainable way to fund your legacy.
Is my money safe?
Security is our infrastructure's foundation. All deposits are held in regulated, principal-protected instruments. We utilize audited stablecoin rails and partner with industry leaders like Bridge and Securitize to ensure asset transparency and safety.

Competition & Rewards

How do I get drafted for the Apex Gauntlet?
Qualification is purely meritocratic. It is based on a weighted mix of your Skill Score (from the Vision App), your Stash Score (your financial commitment), and your Referral Score (your community influence). The top-ranked athletes on the Global Leaderboard are invited to the draft for our live, broadcasted events.
What can I do with FSP Champion Points?
FSP Points are the currency of status. Unlike generic cash-back, these points unlock access to the "Inner Circle" of sports. You can redeem them for signed gear from our partners (like Virat Kohli and Mookie Betts), VIP experiences at global events (E1, F1, World Cup), or "Challenge Tickets" to enter high-stakes tournaments.
Why do I need to deposit to compete?
FSP is built on the belief that athletes should have "skin in the game." While anyone can play for free, depositing unlocks "Pro Tier" status. This gives you higher earning multipliers, more challenge tickets, and eligibility for the biggest prize pools. It signals your commitment to your own journey.
