F.A.Q. Note Specific

Who is eligible to invest?
The FSP Note is available to Accredited Investors within the United States (under Regulation D) and is open to International Investors (Non-US persons) globally (under Regulation S), subject to local jurisdictional compliance.
Is the FSP Note a debt or equity instrument?
It is a Structured Digital Asset. It combines the capital preservation mechanics of a structured note (Debt) with the upside appreciation of a portfolio index (Equity).
How is the "Capital Preservation Strategy" achieved?
A portion of the capital stack is allocated to low-risk, yield-generating stablecoin treasuries (e.g., U.S. Treasury-backed tokens) to stabilize the floor value of the Note, while the remaining capital seeks alpha in sports assets.
Can I trade the Note?
The FSP Note is a digital security issued on the blockchain. While it is designed as a hold-to-maturity asset, it enables peer-to-peer transferability and liquidity within compliant secondary markets (subject to applicable lock-up periods).
