2023 IBF World Bowling Championships

Hosted at the Kuwait Bowling Sporting Club in Salmiya, Kuwait, from October 3 to 15, 2023. Organized by the International Bowling Federation and Kuwait Bowling Federation, the event saw participation from IBF member federations, each registering up to 6 male and 6 female athletes.
